Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation and success will go side by side in new Bangladesh 2.0 making sure a better life of every citizen.

Sustainability

Sustainable uses of resources so that future generations can enjoy a healthy Bangladesh.

Infrastructure

Solid infrastructure facilities for better life and supporting the economy.

Empowerment

New Bangladesh 2.0 represents a vision for a more progressive and inclusive empowerment

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

New Bangladesh 2.0 embodies a transformative vision for the nation's future, focusing on sustainable development, economic growth, and social inclusivity. This initiative aims to harness the potential of technology and innovation to propel the country into a new era, where digital literacy and entrepreneurship play pivotal roles in economic empowerment. With a strong emphasis on education, the initiative seeks to provide equitable access to quality learning opportunities, ensuring that all citizens, particularly women and marginalized communities, can thrive. Furthermore, New Bangladesh 2.0 advocates for environmental sustainability, encouraging eco-friendly practices and green technologies to combat climate change. By fostering a culture of participation and collaboration, this vision aims to create a resilient society where every individual has the opportunity to contribute to and benefit from the nation’s progress.
